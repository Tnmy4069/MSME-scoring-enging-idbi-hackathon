# Overview

IDBI Bank Design System is built for a secure, scalable, regulatory-compliant banking ecosystem covering:

- Retail Banking
- Corporate Banking
- MSME Banking
- Agri Banking
- NRI Banking
- Credit Cards
- Digital Payments
- Loan Management
- Treasury & Investments

The system must communicate:

✅ Trust

✅ Security

✅ Stability

✅ Compliance

✅ Professionalism

❌ Startup vibes

❌ Crypto aesthetics

❌ Over-gamification

❌ Excessive gradients

IDBI's digital ecosystem includes Internet Banking, Mobile Banking, GO Mobile+, Samriddhi Portal, Soft Token, WhatsApp Banking and Corporate Banking services.
Source: https://www.idbi.bank.in

# Design Principles

DO

- Prioritize trust
- Show balances clearly
- Reduce transaction friction
- Maintain auditability
- Ensure accessibility
- Keep workflows simple
- Use flashy fintech animations

DON'T
- Hide important banking actions
- Overuse gradients
- Create cluttered dashboards
